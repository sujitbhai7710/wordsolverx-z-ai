How to Use the Solver

  1. Search for a champion and select them to make a guess.
  2. The champion's properties will appear. Click on each property to cycle through feedback colors based on what the game shows you:
    • Green: Correct match.
    • Orange: Partial match.
    • Red: Incorrect.
    • For Release Year, red also indicates direction (⬆️ newer / ⬇️ older).
  3. Once feedback is set, click "Add Guess to History & Filter".
  4. The list of "Possible Champions" will update based on your feedback.
